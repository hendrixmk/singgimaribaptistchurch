BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF OUR CHURCH

Singgimari Baptist Church, inaugurated on February 27th, 2022, is a dedicated place of worship located in Selsella, West Garo Hills, Meghalaya(Postal code-794105). The church serves as a spiritual sanctuary for its congregation, offering a place for prayer, worship, and community engagement. With a focus on nurturing faith, fellowship, and service, Singgimari Baptist Church plays a central role in the spiritual lives of its members. Its leadership and organization ensure a vibrant and supportive environment for believers, fostering growth and connection within the community.

History

Singgimari Baptist Church was founded by a small group of devoted believers seeking a place of worship in their community.

About us

Our history

Stepping Stones of Faith

Early days & ChallengesIn its initial years, the church started with a small but devoted congregation that gathered regularly for prayer and worship. Despite facing financial and logistical challenges, the faith and determination of the members never wavered. Through contributions from the community and unwavering trust in God’s plan, the church gradually expanded, welcoming more believers and strengthening its spiritual foundation.


Foundation & Inauguration: Singgimari Baptist Church was inaugurated on February 27th, 2022, marking the beginning of its spiritual journey in Selsella, West Garo Hills, Meghalaya (Postal code - 794105).

Place of Worship: Established as a sacred space for prayer and worship, the church serves as a spiritual anchor for the local community.

Community & Faith: From its inception, the church has fostered a sense of unity and faith, bringing believers together for collective worship and fellowship.

Growth & Impact: Over time, Singgimari Baptist Church has become more than just a building—it stands as a symbol of devotion, offering support, guidance, and inspiration to its members.

Legacy & Mission: With a commitment to nurturing spiritual growth and service, the church continues to uphold its mission of fostering faith and strengthening community bonds.

Our vision

Faithful Aspiration

Spiritual Growth: Singgimari Baptist Church envisions a community where faith is nurtured, and individuals grow spiritually through prayer, worship, and service.

Unity & Fellowship: Our vision is to strengthen the bonds of fellowship among believers, creating a loving and supportive Christian community.

Service & Outreach: We strive to extend compassion beyond the church walls, actively engaging in charitable work and helping those in need.

Faith Education: The church is committed to teaching and spreading biblical knowledge, ensuring that members are well-equipped in their faith journey.

Legacy & Future: Looking ahead, Singgimari Baptist Church seeks to remain a guiding light of faith, fostering devotion and serving as a pillar of strength for future generations.

Our values

Moral Compass

Faith & Devotion: At Singgimari Baptist Church, unwavering faith in God is the foundation of our beliefs, guiding our actions and purpose.

Love & Compassion: We strive to show Christ-like love by embracing all individuals with kindness, empathy, and understanding.

Integrity & Honesty: Upholding truth and righteousness, we believe in living with integrity in all aspects of life.

Community & Fellowship: Strengthening relationships through unity, support, and shared worship, we create a welcoming space for all believers.

Service & Commitment: Dedicated to serving God and our community, we actively engage in outreach, charity, and spreading the message of hope.
